# Exchange Rate Reference -- D1 Fuel vs Raw Ores

## D1 Fuel Production

- 275 Water Ice → 75 D1 Fuel (Blueprint 1181, 3s)
- 1 Water Ice = 0.273 D1 Fuel
- 1 D1 Fuel = 3.67 Water Ice

## Hydrated Sulfide Matrix → D1 Fuel (2-step)

- 20 Hydrated Sulfide Matrix → 150 Water Ice + 10 Hydrocarbon Residue (Blueprint 1183, 3s)
- 1 Hydrated Sulfide Matrix = 7.5 Water Ice
- 7.5 Water Ice ÷ 3.67 = 2.05 D1 Fuel
- **1 Hydrated Sulfide Matrix = ~2.05 D1 Fuel**
- **1 D1 Fuel = ~0.49 Hydrated Sulfide Matrix**

## Notes

- Feldspar Crystals and Platinum-Palladium Matrix have no D1 Fuel conversion path. They produce component intermediates (Hydrocarbon Residue, Silica Grains, Iron-Rich Nodules, Palladium) that feed into Thermal Composites, Carbon Weave, and Reinforced Alloys.
- Hydrated Sulfide also produces 10 Hydrocarbon Residue per 20 ore as a byproduct, which has additional value for component production.
- All raw ores share the same portion size (10,000) and mining cycle time, so equal mining effort applies.
